# Knowledge Graph Report - Batch 2 (Lote 2)

## Overview
This batch comprises classical texts, modern interpretations of ancient philosophy, esotericism, and psychoanalytic foundations. The overarching theme connects "Philosophy as a Way of Life" (PWL), the transition from myth/magic to reason, and the deep roots of spiritual/philosophical practices across Eastern and Western traditions.

## Core Thematic Concepts (Nodes)

### 1. The Roots of Western Wisdom & Philosophy
- **Concepts:** Transition from Mythos to Logos, Presocratic Wisdom, Origins of Rationality, Hellenistic Ethics.
- **Key Sources:**
  - *Giorgio Colli* (Sabedoria Grega I & II, O Nascimento da Filosofia)
  - *F.M. Cornford* (Principium Sapientiae)
  - *Marcel Detienne* (Os Mestres da Verdade na Grécia Arcaica)
  - *Diogenes Laertius* (Lives of Eminent Philosophers)
  - *Diodorus Siculus* (Library of History)
  - *Strabo* (Geography)
  - *Euripides* (As Bacantes, Fragments)

### 2. Hellenistic Schools & Practical Ethics
- **Concepts:** Stoicism, Epicureanism, Skepticism, Ataraxia, Eudaimonia.
- **Key Sources:**
  - *Epicurus* (Cartas e Máximas Principais)
  - *Epictetus* (Complete Works)
  - *Gazzinelli* (A Vida Cética de Pirro)

### 3. Orphism, Pythagoreanism & Ancient Magic
- **Concepts:** Mystery Cults, Transmigration of Souls, Ritual Magic, Esoteric Antiquity.
- **Key Sources:**
  - *Alberto Bernabé* (Platão e o Orfismo)
  - *Gazzinelli* (Fragmentos Órficos)
  - *Gabriele Cornelli* (In Search of Pythagoreanism)
  - *Derek Collins* (Magic in the Ancient Greek World)

### 4. Philosophy as a Way of Life (PWL) & Spiritual Exercises
- **Concepts:** Askēsis, Epimeleia Heautou (Care of the Self), Transformation of the Subject, Buddhist/Vedic Practices.
- **Key Sources:**
  - *John M. Cooper* (Pursuits of Wisdom)
  - *Michel Foucault* (A Hermenêutica do Sujeito, História da Sexualidade II)
  - *Ambury* (Philosophy as a Way of Life)

  - *Chase* (Hadot & Critics 2024)
  - *Mircea Eliade* (Yoga: Immortality and Freedom)
  - *S.N. Goenka* (Vipassana Discourse)

### 5. Esotericism, Shamanism & Myth
- **Concepts:** Western Esotericism, Ecstatic Journeys, Cosmologies, Sacred Texts.
- **Key Sources:**
  - *Antoine Faivre* (Western Esotericism)
