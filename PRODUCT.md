# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Readers of "O Significado do Casamento" who want a visual, printable summary of the book.

## Product Purpose
To provide an impeccable, aesthetically pleasing, and highly readable PDF summary (cheat sheet) of the book's chapters.

## Brand Personality
Sophisticated, calm, elegant, editorial, reflective.

## Anti-references
SaaS dashboards, loud marketing, generic corporate templates.

## Design Principles
- Minimalist but not empty
- Editorial typography
- Calm, earthy colors
- Print-optimized clarity

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High contrast text, legible font sizes for printing.
